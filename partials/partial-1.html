VALUE OF MONEY

The value of money can be determined by the demand that it has. We can measure the value of money in three ways: by the exchange rates, treasury notes and through foreign exchange reserves Inflation is when the value of money steadily declines over time. Once people expect that prices will rise, they are more likely to buy now, before prices go higher. That increases demand, which tells producers they can safely pass on more costs.

Deflation is when the value of money increases. That sounds like a great thing, but it is worse for the economy than inflation.

GMO FOOD

The full form of GMO Food is Genetically Modified Organism. Their genetic material has been modified using various methods of genetic engineering.

They are also used for providing resistance to certain pests, diseases, environmental conditions and chemical treatments..

Bacteria are the easiest microorganisms to engineer. They are widely used for research, food production, industrial protein purification and art.

Viruses are used as vectors for inserting genetic information into other organisms. This process is called transduction and the recipient of the introduced DNA becomes a GMO. Different viruses have different efficiencies and capabilities. Researchers can use this to control various factors; like the target location, insert size and duration of gene expression. Any dangerous sequences inherent in the virus should be removed, while those that allow the gene to be delivered effectively are retained.

Fungi is used in making biopesticides. Fungi has the advantage of infecting the insects through a single contact, unlike bacteria and viruses.
